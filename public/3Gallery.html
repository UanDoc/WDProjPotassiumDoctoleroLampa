Uan's favorite type of apple variant is that of the Fuji apple. Did you know? The largest Fuji apple orchard exists in the Loess plateaus of China, especially in the Luochuan (洛川县) county. The Fuji apple variant is known for being sweet, juicy, and crisp; additionally, the Fuji apple variant was actually a crossbreed developed in Japan between the American apple variants of "Red Delicious" and "Ralls Janet".
Supporting local businesses is an important cause that we as Philippine Science High School (PSHS) students should do. We should celebrate the local community for being able to grow apples even in our climate!
The Americas are currently the 2nd largest producers of apples, next to China. Major varieties include Gala, Fuji, Honeycrisp, and Granny Smith.
